Common questions

Why does Pro cost $39.99/year?

Every Pro feature costs me something ongoing — AI generation runs on your device, but the box-score data feed that makes the recaps possible is a paid tier. Pro pays for the data, keeps the free tier truly free, and keeps DeansList independent.

Do you plan to add Women's college basketball?

Not right now. DeansList is deliberately focused on NCAA men's — the focus is what makes the app shippable at this level of polish. Multi-league would un-do that. If you want women's coverage, tell me — I read every request.

Are you affiliated with the NCAA or a sportsbook?

No. DeansList is an independent iOS app. All data comes from the balldontlie NCAAB API. Odds are informational only — no bet placement, no sportsbook checkout, no affiliate router.

How do I restore a Pro subscription on a new device?

Open DeansList → Settings → tap Restore Purchases. Sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. If restore doesn't find the subscription, email me and I'll dig into the specific transaction.

Does DeansList collect my data?

No personal data. Anonymous Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics collect aggregate usage and crash reports — no user identifier, no advertising ID, no linked identity. Full details on the Privacy page.
